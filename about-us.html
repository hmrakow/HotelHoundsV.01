About Us

HotelHounds was founded by a group of dog lovers who were fed up with the difficulties of traveling with our pets. We knew there had to be a better way. And after long nights, lots of doggy treats, and a few long walks, we found it! And now we want to share it with all of you. We are proud to offer our solution, HotelHounds. At HotelHounds, our mission is to make traveling easier and more enjoyable for pets and their parents.

At HotelHounds, you will find everything you need to make traveling with your pet a reality. Through our online platform, you will be able to find pet sitters to watch your pet in your hotel room while you are enjoying the not so pet friendly events on your vacation. You will also be able to find and book hotels that are pet friendly, and find pet friendly venues, events and services at your destination.

Whether you are heading on a family vacation, a work trip, or in search of a little alone time (with your pet of course), we believe bringing your pet should enhance your journey. And with HotelHounds, we believe it can!
