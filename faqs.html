How do I sign up to become a pet sitter?

The following must be completed before consideration to become a pet sitter:

  • Online Application
  • HotelHounds Dog Knowledge test.
  • Brief discussion with a member of HotelHounds team.
  • Provide HotelHounds with at least two references.
  • Completion of online profile.

How do I get approved to become a sitter?

Before becoming a sitter, HotelHounds requires that each applicant meet the following requirements:

  • Must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Pass our test to determine your knowledge of dogs.
  • Receive positive results from the HotelHounds background investigation.
  • Completion of online profile, to include a picture of yourself.

How will I know if I’m approved to be a sitter with HotelHounds?

VHotelHounds will send you a “Welcome to the pack” email 1-3 business days after we receive all of the requested information.

How much is the service fee?

Our service fee is 15% of the total amount of your charge.

How will I identify myself with the Pet Parent at the hotel?

All pet sitters will be provided with a HotelHounds name tag or t-shirt.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency with a pet?

Contact HotelHounds right away. If you are unable to connect with us, contact the pet owner to alert him/her of the situation.

HotelHounds will provide all sitters with a list of nearby veterinary hospitals for you to visit if necessary.

Do I have to pay for veterinary costs?

We recommend that you use good judgment when consulting or visiting a veterinary hospital. Please call HotelHounds if a pet requires treatment and we will then advise you how to proceed. You will need to provide payment for the treatment, but be sure to keep the invoice so it can be submitted for immediate reimbursement.

What do I do if a dog bites me?

If a dog bites you, please contact HotelHounds immediately.

Am I responsible for damage done in the hotel room while I’m pet sitting?

If a dog soils the flooring of a room while you are pet sitting, it is your responsibility to clean up the mess as best as possible.

It is your responsibility to prevent dogs from damaging hotel property while under your care. If a problem does occur, call HotelHounds immediately.

What happens if I have to cancel a Pet Sitting appointment?

If you have to cancel an appointment 12 to 24-hours before the appointment please contact HotelHounds immediately.

If you have to cancel an appointment 0-12 hours before the time of scheduled appointment, please contact HotelHounds immediately. You will be charged a cancellation fee of $10.00.

What happens if a Pet Parent cancels a scheduled appointment?

Each pet sitter will determine his/her own cancellation policy and it will be applied when cancellations occur.

What do I do if I’m going to be late to a scheduled appointment?

If you are running late to an appointment contact the Pet Parent immediately. Contact HotelHounds if a problem arises.

What do I do if the Pet Parent is late returning to the room?

HotelHounds offers a 15-minute grace period for Pet Parents who are late returning to the hotel room. If the Pet Parent returns to the room more than 15-minutes later than the scheduled time, attempt to contact the Pet Parent immediately. If you are unable to get in touch with the Pet Parent, immediately contact HotelHounds for further instruction.

Pet Parents can be held responsible for paying a late fee of $10.00 per every 15-minutes past the scheduled and agreed upon Pet Sitting appointment.

What do I do if I take the dog outside and lock myself out of the hotel room?

Contact the front desk of the hotel to explain the situation and receive a new key. If problems occur, contact HotelHounds.

Inform the Pet Parent of the incident upon his/her return.

Do I have to take the dog for a walk or outside while I’m watching him/her?

Ask the Pet Parent if they would like you to take the dog outside while under your care.

How do I improve my profile?

We have found that the higher the sitter is ranked (based on our 1-5 rating system) and the more information the Pet Sitter provides, the more their services are desired.

  • Be personable and provide the best service possible, in return for high ratings and excellent reviews.
  • Personalize your profile. Add pictures of you with dogs and discuss what makes you a great pet sitter.
  • Provide a reasonable cancellation policy.
  • Provide a flexible schedule.

Do you withhold employment taxes?

No. HotelHounds is not an employment service and is not liable for any type of tax or withholding. If you receive over $600.00 in a calendar year from HotelHounds, you will receive a 1099.
